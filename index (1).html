About Me

From academia to industry — a data career built across three countries

I'm Surangani Bandara, an analyst based in Melbourne whose career has taken a path I wouldn't trade: from university lecture halls and government consulting rooms in Sri Lanka, to building operational dashboards for Australia's disability services sector.

I spent seven years as a Senior Lecturer in Spatial Data Analytics at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, leading applied research, supervising over 50 student projects, and teaching people how to think rigorously about data. Alongside that, I worked as a Data Science Consultant across government and industry — building BI solutions for national disaster management agencies, higher education institutions, and retail organisations. Each engagement taught me something different about how data lives inside organisations and what it takes to make it genuinely useful.

In April 2024 I migrated to Australia and joined Forever Cornerstone, an NDIS provider in Melbourne, as an Insights & Reporting Analyst. Here I design and deploy Power BI dashboards that monitor workforce KPIs, build star-schema data models with DAX and Power Query, implement governance frameworks, and translate complex operational data into clear analytics that support management decisions.

What has stayed constant across every role, every sector, and every country is the same instinct: ask why behind the numbers. A chart is only as valuable as the question it answers. I build dashboards that answer real questions for real people — and I document every assumption along the way.

⭐ Featured Project

NDIS Participant, Budget, Utilisation & Plan Management Insights Dashboard

A 7-page interactive Power BI dashboard analysing 1,784,506 rows of public NDIA quarterly data across 5 quarters (March 2025 – March 2026). Covers participant demographics, plan budgets, support utilisation rates, diagnosis profiles, plan management trends, and geographic breakdowns to service district level. Includes a Data Quality & Methodology page documenting suppression volumes and data governance decisions.
